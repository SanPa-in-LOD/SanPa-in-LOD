Study of the domain and items

Here we describe the main idea of the project and the 10 items that we have chose

The Idea

The community of San Patrignano (Comunità di San Patrignano) is a therapeutic community located in Coriano (Rimini) that was founded by Vincenzo Muccioli in 1978. San Patrignano is acknowledged as one of the first communities that were built in Italy for helping people to recover from drug addiction, and in particular for heroine abuse, which was widely rapidly spreading among youngsters during the 70s and 80s. The community and its founder gained public attention for several reasons, although not all of them were actually benevolent: Vincenzo Muccioli was charged for various crimes, including kidnapping, but he was ultimately declared not guilty. Nowadays San Patrignano is one of the largest communities for addiction recovery in Italy and it hosts more than 1500 people.

The Items

I grouped the items according to the field of relevance, or type of information, they provide to the description of the main object. The movie and the song provide a socio-cultural background of the period under consideration, while the portrait, the interview and the book are insights into Muccioli’s personality and character. The article (which refers to Muccioli’s trial) and the law, moreover, are some events connected to the community, but not directly performed inside it, and thus they offer a panoramic view on the issues revolving around the community, such as drug policies and the debate over patients’ treatments. Finally, an in-depth view on San Patrignano is offered by the picture, the journal and the Netflix documentary: the journal is produced by the people living in the community, while in the Netflix documentary we can find some vivid testimonies of some of the people who worked and lived inside the community in the 70s and 80s, a pivotal time for the community.
