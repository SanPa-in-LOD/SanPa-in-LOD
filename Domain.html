Study of the domain and items

The Idea

The community of San Patrignano was founded in 1978 by Vincenzo Muccioli. San Patrignano is a therapeutic community, helping people recovering from drug addiction, and, during the 70s and 80s, it primarily hosted heroin addicts who were victims of the rampant spread of the substance in Italian cities. San Patrignano is considered as one of the first organizations of its kind and gained special attention for the wholesome, father-like and caring personality of Muccioli. Nevertheless, as the community grew, the attention of the public moved towards some episodes of alleged misconduct inside the community: Muccioli was protagonist of a public debate over the methods of San Patrignano, and was also charged with accusations of kidnapping, of which he was ultimately found not guilty by the court. Nowadays, the community hosts more than 1500 people, making it one of the largest communities for addiction recovery in Italy. Our project focuses on San Patrignano and Muccioli, as they are often perceived as two almost interchangeable entities, at least during the years under consideration, as will be clearer later.

The Items

In order to paint a clear picture of what was said earlier, we grouped the items according to their field of relevance, or type of information they add to the description of the main object. The movie and the song provide a socio-cultural background of the period under consideration, while the portrait, the interview and the book are insights into Muccioli’s personality and character. The article (which refers to Muccioli’s trial) and the law, moreover, are events connected to the community in some way, but which were not actually performed inside it, and thus they offer a panoramic view of the issues revolving around, but outside, the community. Finally, and in-depth view on San Patrignano is offered by the picture, the journal and the Netflix documentary: all these items offer an inside perspective on the topic, in some way or another, for instance through direct testimonies.
