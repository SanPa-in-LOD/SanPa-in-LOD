Conceptual model

Since the nature of our domain is (vasto) and includes various elements we decided to use different ontologies dedicated to CH. In particular We used, RDA and Bibframe for the items (facenti parte) the library domain OAD, EAC-CPF and RIC-O for archival resources. CIDOC-CRM for museum objects. In addition, we used EBUCORE to address specific tasks, RDF/RDFS â€“ for concepts and FOAF (for authority). NMM (Nepomuk Multimedia Ontology), was invece, used to address the multimedia objects Furthermore, to describe a specific item pertaining the legal field we used ELI (European Legislation Identifier) paired with SKOS and XML-Schema. Finally, CIDOC-CRM, FRBRoo and DublinCore were used for harmonization purposes and to create the bone structure of our.
