E/R model

Here we describe the items thorugh the E/R model

The first E/R Model

The first E/R model describes the relationships of the chosen items to the concept under consideration. Some items are directly linked to the community, the main object, while others are linked to Muccioli. Some items are linked to both San Patrignano and Muccioli, showing how the boundaries between the person and the organization are sometimes blurred. For example, the article which refers to San Patrignano and the controversies around it, actually focuses on Muccioliâ€™s trial and persona. In order to make even more visible the strength of the connection between Muccioli and San Patrignano, the link between them has been marked in green in the E/R model.

The second E/R Model

The first E/R model has been enriched by the description of each item linked to the main object. This description, alongside the theoretical model, provide a knowledge base for the creation of the conceptual model. All the links are in the form of a predicate, while the items are usually the subjects. In some cases, the predicate is performed on the item, as is the case of the Jervolino Vassalli Law, where the subj. Muccioli performs the action of influencing the creation of the obj. law.
