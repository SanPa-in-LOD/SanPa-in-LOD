E/R model

Here we describe the items thorugh the E/R model

The first E/R Model

description of the main object and first linking to the 10 items. Some items are directly linked to the main object, the community, while others refer more specifically to Muccioli, the founder and “frontman” of the organization. Some items are linked to both San Patrignano and Muccioli, showing how the boundaries between the person and the organization are sometimes blurred

The second E/R Model

The ER model has been enriched by the description of the items. The description provided is specific and detailed, providing a knowledge base for the creation of the theoretical and the conceptual model. The links are in the form of a predicate while the items are usually the subject. In some case, the predicate is performed on the item, as is the case of the J.V. law, where the subject Muccioli performs the action of influencing the creation of the object law.
