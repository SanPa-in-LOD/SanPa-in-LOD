RDF

In order to describe our project through RDF we chose some items and entities that better represented our core idea. In particular, we focused on the entities that could make explicit the most significant interconnections and relations between all the items and entities in place.

We have minted the URIs of our entities and, when available, connected them with already existing URIs through the owl:sameAs expression. Then we connected our entities through the properties that we considered more representative with the URIs of internal and external resources.

We decided to use the Turtle syntax, since it is the most flexible and simple one to follow.
