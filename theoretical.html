Theoretical

Here we describe the items thorugh the theoretical model. The theoretical model is a description of the domain and the items in the widest and most general sense, so that the description could in principle be applied to each item of the same kind. Every entity has been thus generalized (es: Article regarding San Patrignano: Article) , while the predicates have not changed.
